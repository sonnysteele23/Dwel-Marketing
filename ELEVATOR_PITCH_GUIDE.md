## 💡 How to Use

### For In-Person Presentations
1. Open `elevator-pitch.html` in your browser
2. Use it as speaking notes or display it on screen
3. Walk through each section in order
4. Total presentation time: 60-90 seconds

### For Virtual Meetings
1. Share screen showing `elevator-pitch.html`
2. Scroll through sections as you speak
3. End with contact information visible

### For Printed Handouts
1. Open `elevator-pitch.html` in browser
2. Press `Ctrl+P` (Windows) or `Cmd+P` (Mac)
3. Print as PDF or to printer
4. Distribute to prospects/partners

### For Email
1. Open in browser
2. Take screenshot or save as PDF
3. Attach to emails with pitch

## 📝 Speaking Script

**[5 sec] The Hook:**
"What if you could reduce employee absenteeism by 40%, prevent costly turnover, and boost productivity—while enabling your employees' loved ones to age safely at home?"

**[20 sec] The Problem:**
"Right now, 73% of employees are juggling work with caregiving for aging parents. This is costing the Global 5000 billions in lost productivity, increased absenteeism, and early resignations. Meanwhile, 94% of seniors want to age in place, but without proper support, this creates safety concerns and family burnout."

**[25 sec] The Solution:**
"That's where DWEL comes in. Our integrated AI platform is the only solution that addresses both sides of this challenge. We use 24/7 intelligent monitoring to keep older adults safe at home, which gives employee caregivers peace of mind—reducing absenteeism, preventing resignations, and driving higher productivity."

**[15 sec] The Market:**
"We're targeting Global 5000 companies. With over 1 billion adults aged 60+ globally, and that number growing 5X faster than the general population, this is a multi-billion dollar market that's accelerating rapidly."

**[10 sec] Why Now:**
"By 2050, the U.S. will have 161 million seniors—a 63% increase. Companies need solutions now to retain talent and maintain productivity."

**[10 sec] The Ask:**
"I'd love to discuss how DWEL can help your organization address this challenge. Can we schedule time to explore a partnership?"
