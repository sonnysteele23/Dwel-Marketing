HOW IT WORKS

Simple steps to get started with DWEL

1. Sign Up

Employers can easily add DWEL to their benefits package. Employees create their accounts and set up their caregiving profiles.

2. Assess Needs

Our platform evaluates caregiving requirements and provides personalized recommendations for support services and resources.

3. Access Resources

Connect with verified caregiving services, schedule support, and access educational resources all in one place.

4. Track & Optimize

Monitor care progress, adjust support as needs change, and maintain work-life balance with our intelligent tracking tools.
